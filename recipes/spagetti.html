Pinoy style Spaghetti

Pinoy Chicken Adobo is a popular Filipino dish known for its savory, tangy, and slightly sweet flavor profile. It typically consists of chicken pieces marinated and simmered in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns until tender. The dish is often enjoyed with steamed rice and can be found in various versions across the Philippines, with variations in ingredients and cooking methods depending on regional preferences. It's a comforting and flavorful dish that's beloved by many Filipinos and has gained popularity internationally for its delicious taste and simplicity.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces (such as drumstricks, thighs, or a whole chicken, cut into servinc pieces)
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar (usually cane vinegar or white vinegar)
  • Garlic (minced or crushed)
  • Bay leaves (dried or fresh)
  • Whole black peppercorns (or ground black pepper)
  • Brown sugar (usually vegetable oil)
  • Cooking oil (usually vegetable oil)
  • Water or chicken broth (for simmering)
  • Salt (to taste)
